Common questions

People often search for ways to sign PDFs privately, for free, or without an account. Short answers below; when Air Sign fits, we link to the browser signer.

How can I sign a PDF without uploading my file to a website?

Choose a signer that runs entirely in your browser so the PDF is not sent to a company server just to apply a signature. Air Sign works on your device: open the file, draw your signature in the air with your webcam, place it on the page, and export when you are ready.

Many tools offer a free trial or free tier. Air Sign lets you complete your first signature free in the browser; optional paid slots may apply for additional signatures.

How do I add a handwritten signature to a PDF using my webcam?

Air Sign uses hand tracking. You draw in the air in front of the camera, confirm with a gesture, drag the signature onto the PDF or image, then export. You need a modern browser and a webcam.

Where does my PDF go when I use an online PDF signer?

It depends on the product. Some services upload your file to the cloud. With Air Sign, the signing step runs in your browser on your machine; you download the signed file when you choose. Always read each provider’s privacy policy.

Are electronic signatures on PDFs legally binding?

Laws vary by country and document type. Many routine agreements accept electronic signatures, but regulated contracts may need specific platforms or workflows. Air Sign does not provide legal advice; consult a professional when in doubt.
